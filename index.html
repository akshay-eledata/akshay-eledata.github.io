Our Facilities

Cutting-edge infrastructure designed for exceptional ENT care

State-of-the-Art ENT Outpatient Chambers

Fully equipped, patient-friendly consultation rooms designed for efficient diagnosis and treatment of ENT conditions, with modern equipment and adherence to infection control protocols.

Advanced Audiology Services & Hearing Aid Center

Comprehensive audiology testing using calibrated, evidence-based equipment in sound-treated rooms. Includes a full-service hearing aid dispensing and fitting unit with certified audiologists.

In-House Pharmacy

Licensed pharmacy located within the facility, ensuring seamless access to prescribed medications with proper storage, inventory control, and adherence to national drug safety regulations.

NABH-Compliant Operation Theatres

Modular and fully equipped OTs designed as per the latest NABH guidelines, featuring:

  • HEPA filters and laminar airflow systems & Sterile zoning and access control
  • Emergency power backup and fire safety compliance
  • Regular validation and calibration of surgical equipment
